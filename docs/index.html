Desktop YouTube Intelligence

Turn raw transcripts into briefs, channel strategy, and publish-ready assets.

Cipher Lens is a local-first desktop app for creators, researchers, and operators who need faster clarity from YouTube videos. Fetch transcripts, clean noisy captions, analyze full channels, and export polished reports without depending on a cloud dashboard.

Workflow

From video URL to decision-ready output in one session.

01

Bring in the source

Paste a transcript, import subtitles, or fetch public YouTube captions and metadata.

02

Clean the transcript

Remove speaker labels, dedupe lines, trim fillers, and normalize noisy caption text.

03

Generate structured analysis

Produce takeaways, chapters, keywords, clip moments, content plans, and packaging ideas.

04

Export and reuse

Save briefs locally, export text or PDF files, and reload work later from the built-in library.
